Ayanda Keith Dube

Ayanda Keith Dube is a student and advocate for refugee issues.

He is currently involved with Mapping Memories "a collaborative media project which uses personal stories and a range of media tools (video, sound walks, mapping, photography) to better understand the experiences of youth with refugee experience in Montreal" (Mapping Memories).

He has worked with the Canadian Council for Refugees on a number of campaigns which have taken us to Ottawa to lobby for the granting of permanent residence to non-status residents living in Canada for up to 10 years in limbo, while working on a BA double major in economics and political science with the hope of going to law school after completion of his undergraduate degree. Last year, he was chosen as a CBC Montrealer the Week for his work with Mapping Memories in the high schools of Montreal.
